About the Program

The Agriculture Project at Kola Girls Secondary School is a core part of our practical learning curriculum. It is designed to help students gain real-world experience in sustainable agriculture while promoting environmental awareness and responsibility.

In the 2025 KCSE Agriculture Project, all students took part in a comprehensive tree planting initiative. Working in groups, they prepared land, planted various types of trees, and monitored growth over time. This hands-on approach allowed them to apply classroom knowledge to actual farm-based activities.

The main goals of the program are to:

  • Promote sustainable farming techniques among students
  • Enhance environmental conservation and reforestation efforts
  • Equip learners with agribusiness and teamwork skills
  • Foster a sense of responsibility and care for the environment

This program has made a lasting impact not just on the students, but on the entire school community. It encourages innovation, collaboration, and pride in hands-on achievements. Each group documented their journey through photos and videos to showcase their work and share their stories.
